Welcome to Helena!

Helena, Montana’s capital city, is a picturesque destination nestled between mountain ranges. Known for its gold rush history, the city features Victorian-era architecture, museums, and scenic views. Helena’s compact downtown offers art galleries, unique shops, and easy access to hiking trails, making it an ideal place for visitors who appreciate history and outdoor adventure.
