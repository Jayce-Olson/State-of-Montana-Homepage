Welcome to Missoula!

Missoula is a dynamic college town known for its breathtaking natural scenery and vibrant arts community. Nestled in the Northern Rockies, Missoula boasts an energetic outdoor lifestyle, from hiking in the surrounding mountains to rafting on the Clark Fork River. It also serves as the cultural hub of western Montana, offering visitors an eclectic mix of galleries, breweries, and historic sites.
